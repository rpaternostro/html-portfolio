The Best Movies According to Rodrigo

My Top 10 Movies of All Time

  1. Interstellar

    A Christopher Nolan masterpiece that brilliantly integrates science and physics.

  2. Robinson Crusoe

    A powerful movie that taught me the true value of friendship.

  3. Forrest Gump

    A beautiful and artistic film with a touching story.

  4. Batman Trilogy (Christopher Nolan)

    Impossible to choose just one — the whole trilogy is a cinematic achievement.

  5. Inception

    I’m still confused by the ending. Brilliant and mind-bending.

  6. The Last Samurai

    An inspiring story that teaches valuable life lessons.

  7. The Lion King

    My childhood favorite — I’ve watched it more than 50 times. Hakuna Matata!

  8. Back to the Future

    Yes, I know... I'm a nerd, and I love it!

  9. Butterfly Effect

    Makes me reflect on how every decision — every yes or no — can change our lives completely.

  10. Star Wars

    Did I mention I’m a nerd? Hahaha!
